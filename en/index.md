Zhijun Gao is a Lecturer and Master's Supervisor at the School of Software & Microelectronics, Peking University. He serves as a Board Member of IEEE Professional Communication Society, Secretary-General of the Subcommittee on Computer-Aided Terminology Work under the National Technical Committee on Language and Terminology Standardization, and Secretary-General of the Technical Communication Service Committee of the China Association for Standardization.

He is currently leading several interdisciplinary projects at the intersection of language and AI, including "Construction of Multilingual Terminology Database for Traditional Chinese Medicine Cultural Audio-Visual Programs Based on Large Language Models" (National Language Commission) and "Construction of Large-Scale Tibetan Synthetic Datasets" (China Tibetology Research Center). He has undertaken projects such as the "Aerospace and Defense Simplified Technical English (ASD-STE) Rule Checker" and the development of the "Human Health and Wellness Corpus" for a major Social Science Fund project. He has also led the development of multiple standards including "User Experience Assessment Specification for Technical Documentation."

He values the integration of academic research with industry practice, and has collaborated extensively with leading companies such as Xiaomi, Alibaba, and Baidu in translation technology and technical writing.
