## Project Overview

Provide an online MOOC for master's students in health communication, while building a bilingual parallel corpus for student research.

### Project Duration

December 30, 2022 - September 1, 2023

### Development Team

- Zhijun Gao
- Bing Shuai

### Corpus Specifications

In terms of corpus scale, the corpus collected a total of 3,202 Chinese-English documents (data as of May 2023), with a total character count of 3,956,595 characters, including 2,633,736 Chinese characters and 1,322,859 English characters.
