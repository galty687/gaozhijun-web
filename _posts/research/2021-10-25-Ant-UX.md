## 开发者的文档体验研究

开发者文档在软件开发过程中扮演着重要的角色，从软件的安装到故障的排除，没有文档的话，开发工作寸步难行。当下以用户为中心的设计理念，主要是以最终用户为中心，而以开发者为中心设计其开发体验的尝试则较少。本研究尝试从文档体验的角度提升开发体验，从而让开发者因为良好的信息体验形成对开发技术的粘性。

- 开发者的信息旅途是怎样的？
- 如何设计文档的体验？
- 如何评估文档的质量？

## Developer Documentation Experience Research

Developer documentation plays a vital role in the software development process. From software installation to troubleshooting, development work can hardly proceed without documentation. Current user-centered design philosophies primarily focus on end users, with fewer attempts to design the development experience centered on developers. This research attempts to improve the development experience from the perspective of documentation experience, thereby fostering developer loyalty to development technologies through a positive information experience.

## Research Team:

- Zhijun Gao
- Jiangying Wang
- Jincheng Huang
- Wenxi Du

## Research Questions:

- What are the characteristics of Chinese developers?
- What types of software development documentation exist?
- What does the developer's information journey look like?
- How to design the documentation experience?
- How to evaluate documentation quality?
