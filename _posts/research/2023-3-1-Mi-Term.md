## 项目内容
1. 使用小米已有的术语词条训练专门的术语提取神经网络；
2. 使用训练好的神经网络从小米官网、内部材料及待译材料发现术语；
3. 从发现的术语中提取未收录词汇。

- 张玉杰
- 蔺晓洋

## Project Description
1. Training a specialized terminology extraction neural network using Xiaomi's existing terminology entries;
2. Using the trained neural network to discover terminology from Xiaomi's official website, internal materials, and materials pending translation;
3. Extracting uncatalogued terms from the discovered terminology.

### Team:
- Zhijun Gao
- Yujie Zhang
- Xiaoyang Lin


### Research Output
One paper published: "[Enterprise Terminology Extraction Practice Based on Large Language Models](https://www.term.org.cn/CN/10.12339/j.issn.1673-8578.2025.04.031)"
The paper was published in the July 2025 issue of *Chinese Science and Technology Terms Journal*.
