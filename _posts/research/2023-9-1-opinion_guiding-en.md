Project No.: 23AXW008

2023 National Social Science Fund Key Project: Mechanisms, Pathways, and Methods of Public Opinion Guidance in Public Emergencies

Funding: 350,000 RMB

## Project Content

Four specific components:

1. Theoretical model construction for public emergency opinion guidance mechanisms (150,000-word research report); 3-5 CSSCI journal papers;

2. Decision model for public emergency opinion guidance (advisory version)
3. Public emergency information release corpus and terminology keyword list technical generation system (using public health emergencies as an example)
4. One set of multimodal message generation toolkit for grassroots community risk communication and opinion guidance

### Project Duration

2023 to 2026 (three years)



## Participation Content

Responsible for using large language models to generate content, and researching how to influence audience opinions through generated content.
